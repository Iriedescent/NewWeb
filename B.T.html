Brain Teasers

Here are are some of my favorite brain teasers. Try to answer this question and highlight the space after the paragraph to see the answer key.

  1. There is a man found dead in a circular mansion. The detective interviews the cook, maid, and babysitter. The cook said he couldn't have done it because he was preparing the meal. The maid said she couldn't have done it because she was dusting the corners. The babysitter said she couldn't because she was playing with the children. Who was lying?
    Answer: The maid. There are no corners in a circular mansion.

  2. A man went into a party and drank some of the punch. He then left early. Everyone at the party who drunk the punch subsequently died of poisoning. Why did the man not die?
    Answer: The poison from the punch came from the ice cubes. When the man drank the punch, the ice was fully frozen. Gradually, as the ice cubes melted the poison was released into the punch.
  3. A man and a woman live peacefully in a house together. But one day the woman shoots her husband. Then she holds him under water for over five minutes. Finally, she hangs him, but ten minutes later they go out and enjoy a wonderful dinner. How can this be?
    Answer: The woman is a photographer. She shot a picture of her husband, developed it and hung it up to dry.

  4. A rooster lays an egg on the top of the roof. Which way does it roll?
    Answer: Roosters don’t lay eggs.

  5. There is a fire in a 12 storey building. A man panics and jumps out of the window. How does he survive?
    Answer: He jumped from a first floor window.
